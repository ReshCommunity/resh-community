## Emerging Trends

### 1. Layer 2 Dominance
**Prediction**: L2s capture 40%+ of DeFi TVL by June 2025
**Drivers**: Lower fees (100x cheaper), ecosystem maturity, improved UX

### 2. Real-World Asset Expansion
**Prediction**: RWA TVL reaches $5B+ by June 2025
**Areas**: Tokenized treasuries, credit underwriting, real estate

### 3. Restaking & EigenLayer
**Prediction**: Restaking TVL exceeds $15B by June 2025
