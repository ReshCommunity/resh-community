## Outreach Email Template

### Subject Line Options:
1. "Suggested Resource for [Site Name] [Topic] Section"
2. "Comprehensive [Topic] Resource for Your Readers"
3. "[Topic] Guide: Potential Addition to Your Resources"

### Email Body:

```
Subject: Suggested Resource for [Site Name] DeFi Resources

Hi [Name or "Team"],

I came across your [Topic] resource page at [URL] and found it incredibly comprehensive for your community.

I noticed you cover [specific topic they cover], and I thought you might be interested in another resource that could add value to your readers.

I've created a [Hub Name] that provides:

• [Key benefit 1 - e.g., 20+ comprehensive guides on DeFi fundamentals]
• [Key benefit 2 - e.g., Regular updates with latest protocol information]
• [Key benefit 3 - e.g., Beginner-friendly learning paths]

URL: https://resh.community/[hub-slug]

This hub is regularly updated with the latest [topic] information and includes practical guides on all major [topic] areas.

Would you consider adding it to your resources? I'm happy to provide any additional information or create custom content for your audience.

Thanks for your time and for the valuable resource you're building for the [topic] community.

Best regards,
Marc
Resh Community
https://resh.community
```

## Outreach Best Practices

1. **Personalize every email** - Reference specific content on their site
2. **Research contacts** - Find specific people, not generic info@
3. **Provide value first** - Offer something before asking
4. **Keep it brief** - Respecting their time
5. **Follow up respectfully** - 2-3 touches max
6. **Track everything** - Document all interactions
7. **Test different subject lines** - See what gets opens
8. **Time emails well** - Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm recipient time
9. **Mobile-friendly** - Many read on phones
10. **Build relationships** - Not just about the link
