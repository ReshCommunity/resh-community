## Design Specifications

### Colors:
- **Primary**: #627EEA (Ethereum purple-blue)
- **Secondary**: #14F195 (DeFi green)
- **Accent**: #F3BA2F (Gold for highlights)
- **Background**: #1a1a2e (Dark blue-gray)
- **Text**: #ffffff (White)

### Typography:
- **Headings**: Montserrat or Poppins (Bold, 700)
- **Body**: Inter or Roboto (Regular, 400)
- **Numbers**: Poppins (Bold for stats)

### Icon Set:
- Charts/Graphs
- Cryptocurrency logos
- Upward trend arrows
- Shield (security)
- Users (community)
- Calendar (timeline)
